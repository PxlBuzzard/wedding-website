How we met

Nikky and Daniel met through some mutual friends at a few social gatherings some years ago, but nothing much happened outside of the getting to know you phase. In early 2017, Nikky and Daniel reconnected on a dating site with Nikky liking Daniel's profile. Conviently, Nikky and Daniel were already Facebook friends which helped them kick off their first conversation. Within a week they met at a Starbucks, at which point Daniel realized he would be looking down for the rest of his life. Their first date was at Starbucks, which Nikky was a barista for at the time! They went on many dates including Chihuly Glass Muesum and the Kirkland waterfront (which ended up being where the engagement photos were).

As the end of the year approached and Daniel scrambled to figure out how the heck to propose to this perfect woman, the thought of returning to the glass museum for New Year's Eve celebrations was too good to pass up. A couple hours before midnight they snuck outside in the freezing cold weather and Daniel refused to share his coat because a photographer was about to take pictures of the proposal, but Nikky won't let Daniel leave out Daniel's inconsideration for her well-being in this story.
